In the anime, Jessie met her long time partner before she went to Team Rocket, which was a Blissey. When Jessie, James, and Meowth were given food by Blissey she was blamed. However, Jessie stood up and said that she stole the food to save Blissey. Nurse Joy used to have a Chansey that evolved into Blissey. All Nurse Joys in Alola use a Blissey.
