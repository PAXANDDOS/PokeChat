Mothim is based off of a moth. He has yellow wings with orange on the outer and inner tips. His antennae are white, with orange on top. His eyes are orange. His base body is black, with an orange stripe, and white legs.Mothim has the Abilities Swarm and Tinted Lens. He also can be used in Sky Battles. Swarm raises the power of Bug type moves when Mothim's HP is low.
